# Stem: Story-driven job workspaces (invoice_ops)

## Claim

Finance Operations is queue work: approval, pay, dispute — not invoice admin lists.

## Reconstruct

- All ops personas default: finance_ops = metrics + funnel + approval/pay/dispute queues.
- Payment health stays as chart support, not the primary job.

## Not this

- Persona lands on a bare entity list when the job is triage, review, or oversight.
- Story `given:` workspace names that disagree with `default_workspace`.

## Expressions

- `dsl/` workspaces + personas; `docs/guides/story-to-composition.md`
